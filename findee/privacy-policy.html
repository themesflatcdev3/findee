1. Introduction
Welcome to Findee, an online job portal dedicated to helping job seekers, freelancers and employers connect with each other efficiently. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our services.
2. Information We Collect
a. User Profile Information: When you create an account, we collect personal information, including your name, email address, password, and optionally, additional information such as your phone number, resume, location and professional qualifications.
b. Job Search and Application Data: We collect data related to your job search activities, including job preferences, application history, and saved job listings.
c. Employer Information: If you are an employer, we collect information about your company, job listings, location and candidate interactions.
d. Communication Data: We may collect data from your communications with other users, such as messages, interviews, and feedback.
e. Usage Information: We collect data about how you use Findee, including your interactions with the platform, search queries, and device information.
f. Log Data: We may collect log data, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, to monitor platform performance and security.
3. How We Use Your Information
a. Personalization: We use your information to personalize your job search experience, providing job recommendations, newsfeed content, and relevant communications.
b. Communication: We facilitate communication between job seekers and employers, including messages, interview scheduling, and application status updates.
c. Platform Improvement: We use data to improve our platform, develop new features, and enhance user experience.
d. Security: Your information is crucial for platform security and to prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
4. Data Sharing
a. Public Profiles: Some information you provide may be visible to other users, such as your profile, resume, and application history.
b. Employer Access: Employers can access your profile and application information if you apply for their job listings.
c. Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers to support our operations, including hosting, data analysis, and marketing.
d. Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, users, or safety.
5. Your Choices
a. Profile Editing: You can review and edit your profile information at any time.
b. Communication Preferences: You can manage your communication preferences, including email notifications.
c. Data Access and Deletion: You have the right to request access to your data or its deletion, subject to applicable laws.
6. Security
We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data; however, no method is entirely secure. Please take steps to keep your account information safe, including password protection.

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve. We will notify you of significant changes through the platform or by email.
